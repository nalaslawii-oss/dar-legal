Privacy Policy — Dar App

Last updated: July 3, 2026 · النسخة العربية

At "Dar" we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

1. Data we collect

Browsing properties requires no sign-in and collects no personal data about you. Your favorites list is stored locally on your device only. When you sign in (with Apple or Google) we receive only your name and email, to create your account and let you send and track inquiries. When you send an inquiry or booking request about a property, we store the inquiry text, requested booking dates and your phone number (if provided) so the broker can reply. When a listing is added (a broker-only feature), the listing data and photos are stored on our servers to be shown to app users. We do not collect your device location, advertising identifiers, or use any tracking or analytics tools.

2. Photos

Photos attached to a listing are chosen through the operating system's photo picker; the app never accesses your full photo library.

3. Contacting the advertiser

The call and WhatsApp buttons open your phone or WhatsApp app directly; the app does not record any information about these contacts.

4. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell or share your data with any third party. Listing data is hosted with our database provider (Supabase) for operational purposes only, and maps are displayed via the operating system's map service (Apple Maps or Google Maps).

5. Retention and account deletion

Listing data remains as long as the listing is published. You can permanently delete your account and all your data inside the app: Account → Delete account permanently. You may also request deletion by email and we will complete it within 30 days.

6. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the date above reflects the latest revision.

8. Contact

For any privacy inquiry: dar.app@outlook.com
