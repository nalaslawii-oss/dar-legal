Delete Account & Data — Dar App

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · النسخة العربية

The "Dar" (Find Dar) app provides two ways to permanently delete your account and all associated data:

1. In-app deletion (instant)

  1. Open the Dar app and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to the Account tab.
  3. Tap Delete account permanently and confirm.

Your account and all data linked to it (name, email, phone number, inquiries, booking requests) are deleted immediately and irreversibly.

2. Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, email find.dar@outlook.com from the email registered to your account with the subject "Account deletion request". We will delete your account and all data within 30 days and confirm it to you.

3. What is deleted and what may be retained

Permanently deleted: your name, email, phone number, and all of your inquiries and booking requests.

Retained only where legally required: we may keep limited transaction records if required by applicable law in the State of Kuwait, for no longer than the statutory period, after which they are deleted.

Note: browsing listings and the favorites list do not require an account; favorites are stored on your device only.

Contact
